# Product +ive v1.3.1 — The SRS & Widget Release 🚀

## 🧠 Master Your Memory with SRS
This release introduces the **Spaced Repetition System (SRS)**, a cognitive-science-backed engine designed to ensure you never forget what matters.

### Spaced Repetition Engine
- **SM-2 Algorithm**: Intelligent scheduling that predicts when you're about to forget a concept.
- **Active Recall Engine**: Dedicated review sessions with difficulty grading (1-5).
- **SQLite V3 Persistence**: Your flashcards and session blurts are now safely stored in a durable local database.

## 📱 Productive+ at a Glance
Your focus status is now visible directly from your Home Screen with our new premium widgets.

### Home Screen Widgets
- **XP Progress Ring**: Track your weekly point goals without opening the app.
- **Continuous Streak**: Stay motivated with a real-time tally of your daily focused days.
- **Instant Snapshots**: Widgets update automatically as soon as you earn points or finish a ritual.

## 🛠 Stability & Engineering
We have hardened the application core to ensure a seamless production experience.

- **Hardened Signing**: Resolved Android installation issues with a new, robust CI/CD signing pipeline.
- **LSP Cleanup**: Eliminated hidden character corruption in core engines, ensuring 100% code integrity.
- **Dependency Guard**: Aligned modern Swift-UI DSL components for pixel-perfect widget rendering on all Android devices.
